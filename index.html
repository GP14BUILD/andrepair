Technology Meets Tradition

The modern day GP14 is a two person competitive racing dinghy with spinnaker, constructed from either epoxy resin or wood. The fleet enjoys a competitive international, national and regional racing circuit for adult and youth sailors and is active in training initiatives.

This hull design proved its championship pedigree by winning the GP14 World Championship in Skerries in 2022. Boat No. 14262 took first place under Ian Dobson and Andy Tunnicliffe.

Every boat is built to order with custom fit-out options, including through-deck sheeting and Sapelle decks. Spray-finished with only the best epoxy, varnish and paint, each boat is RCD-compliant. We use only FSC®-certified woods from sustainable sources—because performance should never compromise the planet.
